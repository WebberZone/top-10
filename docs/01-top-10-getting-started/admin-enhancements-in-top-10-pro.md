Top 10 Pro enhances your WordPress admin experience with features like a dedicated admin bar menu, customizable dashboard visibility, control over post type columns.

### New Admin Bar Menu

The new admin bar menu provides quick access to essential Top 10 features. You can view daily, total, and overall post counts, navigate directly to Top 10 admin pages, and clear the Top 10 cache – all from the admin bar. This enhancement saves time by putting vital information and controls at your fingertips.

### Customizable Admin Columns

Take control over your admin interface by choosing which post types display Top 10 data columns. This feature allows you to customize the admin screen, giving you a clearer overview of your most popular content across different post types, enhancing your content management experience.

### User Role Control for Dashboard Access

Decide who gets access to Top 10 analytics data with the new user role control feature. You can now specify which user roles are permitted to view the Top 10 dashboard. This enhancement gives you greater control over data visibility and access, ensuring only the right team members see the analytics. The free version only allows Admins to see the dashboard.

### Advanced Taxonomy Filters

Use the `display_only_on_tax_ids` parameter to fine-tune where your popular posts are displayed. This feature allows you to restrict the display of popular posts to specific taxonomy terms, such as categories or tags, giving you more precise control over your content's visibility.

### Enhanced Edit Post Meta Box

Gain more granular control over which posts are featured as popular with the new option in the Edit Post meta box. You can now include popular posts from specific categories directly from the post editing screen, ensuring the right content is highlighted on your site. This only applies for the page that is being viewed.

These admin enhancements provide a more streamlined and flexible experience, allowing you to manage your popular posts more effectively.
